About Me

Hung Sheng Tsai is currently pursuing his Bachelor of Science in Chemistry in National Taiwan University (NTU).
He is also in charge of the Demonstrative Experiment Department in NTU Chemistry Camp, where he explains the underlying concepts and theories of experiments to senior high school students.

Future Prospects

Hung Sheng Tsai believes that the mystery of nature can eventually be revealed by the integrative efforts of human intelligence.
However, due to the complexity and the variety of species, he dedicates himself to analyzing the problem from the elementary aspect: the chemical reactions in organisms.
Fascinated by the delicate structure and the symmetric beauty of natural matters, he is enthusiastic about developing a fundamental law applicable for nature that provides a peaceful coexistence among all species.
